About me

I have a degree in Digital Games from PUCPR and experience in game development. My portfolio demonstrates my creativity and dedication, with a focus on producing high-quality games. I have participated in several game jams, including a second place in the BGS Game Jam in 2019, which allowed me to put my skills into practice and challenge my creativity. I am currently seeking new opportunities as a game developer to contribute with my experience and add value to projects. I hope to create engaging and immersive game experiences for new projects.

Feel free to get in contact!

Projects

Stick it Together (2020)

Platform: PC

Keywords: Casual; Puzzle; Unity3D;

Game developed during Global Game Jam 2020. Here we follow the short story of two friends who accidentally made a mess inside their house and must fix everything together with super glue before their mother returns home.
We recommend you to play this game with a friend!

Fluffy Skirmish (2019)

Platform: PC

Keywords: Online multiplayer; 3rd person shooter; Unity3D;

Game developed during college. Fight against other players as a cute bunnie with a plastic pistol that shoots bubble gums.
Locate yourself by looking at the map and scouting your surroundings. Be careful: if you fail to get out of the danger zone in time, you'll fall into a pit of lava.

itch.io page

Supermarket Battle (2019)

Platform: PC

Keywords: Local multiplayer; PvP; Unity3D.

Game developed in BGS Jam 2019 (we won 2nd place!). In this game you play against your friends and whoever gets the most items from the supermaket wins.
There are no rules: you can crash the others' carts to drop their items or even steal their whole cart to yourself.

Home Sea Home (2019)

Platform: Android

Keywords: Singleplayer; Side Scrolling; Endless runner; Unity3D;

Game developed during Global Game Jam 2019. Before returning to his beloved our sailor protagonist swore to save the seas and its riches from pirates.
Help him complete his arduous task and perhaps you'll be rewarded with a place in our high score board.

Princess Defense (2018)

Platform: PC

Keywords: Singleplayer; Tower defense; Unity3D;

Game developed during college. Play as a brave princess who must protect her castle and the royal jewel from invading orcs.
Be smart, plant traps and you may survive through all enemy waves.
